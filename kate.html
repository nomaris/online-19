Kate Zibluk

Kate Zibluk is a college student that majors in Theater at Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts. While her passion is on the stage (or behind it as she has also done stage managing work), she finds herself embroidering away the boredom of quarantine. She goes with the flow as to not create her hobby a stressful task, resulting in really personalized embroideries.
