Victoria Heffron

Victoria Heffron is a college student that majors in Global Studies at Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts. As her time was freed due to the quarantine, she was able to explore one her passions, that being to film and edit videos which was something that she previously didn't have much time for. She has uploaded these videos to her youtube channel, which up until now did not have much in it. The video about her trip to Cuba was filmed prior to quarantine but was edited during this time.
